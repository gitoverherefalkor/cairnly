## 1. What is the Referral Programme?

The Cairnly Referral Programme lets existing paying Cairnly users invite others to use Cairnly. For each new user who creates a paid Cairnly account using your unique referral code, you receive a reward. Rewards take the form of additional platform features and/or a partial refund of the amount you originally paid for Cairnly.

## 2. Eligibility

You may participate in the Referral Programme if all of the following apply:

- You hold a paid Cairnly account purchased for the standard price (currently EUR 59, or EUR 39 during applicable promotional periods).
- You are at least 18 years of age.
- Your original purchase was made less than six (6) months ago.
- Your account is in good standing and has not been suspended or terminated.

## 4. What counts as a Valid Referral

A referral becomes a Valid Referral only when all of the following conditions are satisfied:

- The referred person creates a paid Cairnly account using your unique referral code.
- The referred person has paid the full applicable purchase price.
- Fourteen (14) days have passed since that payment, during which the referred person has not exercised their statutory right of withdrawal under the EU Consumer Rights Directive.
- The referred person has not initiated a chargeback, payment dispute, or other reversal of their payment.

Your reward is automatically activated or paid out only once all of the above conditions are met. Cairnly may, at its discretion, delay a payout where additional verification is reasonably required.

## 5. How partial refunds are processed

Partial refunds are issued through our payment provider, Stripe, and are returned to the original payment method used for your Cairnly purchase. Refunds are processed as credit notes against the original transaction. The corresponding VAT correction is handled automatically in our accounting.

Refunds typically appear in your account within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer. If your original payment method is no longer valid (for example, an expired or closed card), we will contact you to arrange an alternative payout via bank transfer.

After this period, your referral code is deactivated and no further rewards can be earned, but any rewards already earned are retained.
